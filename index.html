Herbert is more than a loser!

Herbert Blood of a Quease is a game about a man named HERBERT who has a wife named MARJORY. HERBERT is a BLACK TRUCK GANG member who is known for causing mischief around town like robbing the abondoned FACTORY! HERBERT's life of stealing parts of old machines and driving a black truck around is perfect until a BLACK TRUCK GANG member, SCUM MOUTH JOHN threatens to turn HERBERT in to the po-po! HERBERT must straighten up and fight SCUM MOUTH JOHN or go to the big house for life!
